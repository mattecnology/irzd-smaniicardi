Privacy Policy for Irz Düşmanı Icardi

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our 2D runner game, Irz Düşmanı Icardi.

1. Information We Collect

  • Player name: Entered voluntarily by the user for high score leaderboard display.
  • High scores: Saved locally or on our server to maintain the leaderboard.
  • IP address: May be automatically collected by the server for technical and security reasons.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the collected data solely to display player names and scores in the leaderboard. The IP address is used for basic server operations and is not used for tracking or profiling users.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. All data is used internally to provide a better gaming experience.

4. Children’s Privacy

This application is not targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
